About the organisation

SHREE SANT NAGEBABA MULTISTATE CO. OP. CREDIT SOCIETY LTD. AHMEDNAGAR

Sant Nagebaba Multistate offers its customers the option to choose from a wide range of savings accounts with various features and benefits. It is an important source of financial and technical assistance to developing rural areas. Our mission is to help people help themselves and their environment by providing resources

1.OUR VISION

To make every household self-reliant, prosperous, happy and financially literate

2.OUR MISSION

Harmony, stability and economic upliftment through cooperation in India

3.OUR VALUES

The five sutras are trust, security, self-discipline, keeping one's word and keeping time

4.OUR B-HAG

By the year 2030, the organization will add 50 lakh members

5.BRAND PROMISE

Working 365 days for excellent customer service

6.CORE PURPOSE

To enhance the credit and reputation of the members
